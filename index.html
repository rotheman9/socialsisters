About Us

Founded by a team of creative women who believe in the power of beautiful, inclusive design, Social Sister Collective brings a fresh perspective to party styling across Oregon. We specialize in neutral palettes that celebrate every skin tone and create safe, welcoming spaces for all celebrations.

Our commitment to safety-first installations means every setup is carefully planned and executed. From intimate baby showers to large brand activations, we approach each project with the same attention to detail and passion for creating memorable moments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical lead time for bookings?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for most events. For larger installations or brand activations, we suggest 4-6 weeks to ensure availability and proper planning.

Do you offer delivery and installation services?

Yes! We provide full-service delivery, installation, and breakdown for all our packages. Our team handles everything from setup to cleanup, so you can focus on enjoying your event.

Are your balloons eco-friendly?

We use high-quality latex balloons that are biodegradable and sourced from responsible suppliers. We also offer balloon recycling programs and always clean up thoroughly after events.

Can you match specific color schemes?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom color matching and can work with your brand colors, wedding palette, or any specific aesthetic you have in mind. Just let us know your vision during consultation.
