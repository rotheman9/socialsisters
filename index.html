Our Story

Created by women,
for every celebration.

Founded by a team of creative women who believe in the power of beautiful, inclusive design, Social Sisters Collective brings a fresh perspective to party styling across Oregon.

We specialize in neutral palettes that celebrate every skin tone and create safe, welcoming spaces for all celebrations — from intimate baby showers to large-scale brand activations.

🌿

We use biodegradable latex balloons and offer recycling programs at every event.

🤝
